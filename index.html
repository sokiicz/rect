How to play

Divide the whole grid into rectangles. Each rectangle must contain exactly one number, and that number equals the rectangle's area (width × height).

  • Desktop: click & drag to draw a rectangle. Right-click a box to remove it. Scroll to zoom; Shift- or middle-drag to pan.
  • Touch: tap a start cell, then the opposite corner to draw — tap the same cell again to cancel. Pinch to zoom, two-finger drag to pan.
  • Remove: long-press a box, right-click it, or use Erase mode. Drawing over an existing box replaces it.
  • A correct box colours its number; a red dashed outline means it's the wrong size.
