How to play

Divide the whole grid into rectangles. Each rectangle must contain exactly one number, and that number equals the rectangle's area (width × height).

  • Desktop: click & drag to draw a rectangle; click a single cell for a 1×1. Right-click to remove.
  • Touch: tap a start cell, then tap the opposite corner — tap the same cell for a 1×1.
  • Remove: long-press a rectangle, right-click it, or use Erase mode. Drawing over an existing box replaces it.
  • A correct box colours its number; a red dashed outline means the box is the wrong size for its number.
